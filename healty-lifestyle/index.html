Healthy Lifestyle

Nutrition

Balanced meals with whole foods, adequate protein, fiber, and micronutrients support performance and long-term health.

Exercise

Combine strength training, cardio, and mobility work for an efficient, sustainable routine.

Sleep

7–9 hours of consistent, high-quality sleep improves recovery, cognition, and mood.

Tips (List)

  1. Prioritize protein and fiber in meals.
  2. Train 3–5×/week; walk daily.
  3. Keep a regular sleep schedule.
  4. Hydrate; limit ultra-processed foods.
  5. Track progress and adjust gradually.
